My biggest Influences

These are some intellectuals and writers which have shaped the way I look at the world today.
Carl Jung

This is Carl Jung, he is the founder of analytical psychology who wrote work discussing man's 'Collective Unconsious' as well as defining the psychological term 'Archetype' as well as man's 'Shadow'.
Joseph Campbell

Joseph Campbell is the creator of the Heroes Journey, the main influence on classic movie trilogy Star Wars as well as single-handedly creating a comprehensive guide to comparative mythology across his works and life-time.
Alan Watts

Alan Watts is well known for bringing teachings of Eastern Buddhism, Zen and Hinduism to the West, he has written several books and done countless lectures on the subject changing the Western narrative to a more rounded view on cosmology and religion.
Manly P. Hall

Manly P Hall was a member of the Hermetic Order of the Golden Dawn as well as the founder of one of the largest esoteric antiquarian book collections in America called the Philsophical Society. Hall wrote varios esoteric books including the famous, Secret Teachings of All Ages.
